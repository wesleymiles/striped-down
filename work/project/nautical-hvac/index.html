Situation

A client was going through a name change and needed a website. They came to us with a new logo and a brochure. We had a video call where I learned how far they wanted to lean into the theme. Turns out; pretty far. Their main concern was easing their loyal customers into the new brand.

Solution

A custom site with macro-patterns sourced from our agengies pattern library and micro-patterns customized to the client. The pairing of patterns allow for an impressive amount of flexibility.

A rich style guide based on their brochure and logo. The styles could now be applied to other forms marketing. They even got a custom brand for their maintenance plan.

One thing that would improve the design is professional photography. Stock photos can only do so much to build trust.

“Even the previous website company saw it and said how awesome the website was.”
