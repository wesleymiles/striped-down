What’s this about the Gift-Economy?

I’m experimenting with a radical form of doing business -- a method of exchange based on trust.

Here’s how it works:

  1. I promise to deliver your project to the best of my ability, within scope, and within the timeline.
  2. You agree to offer what feels right from your end. Some upfront, some in the middle, some at the end.

No formal contracts. Just a consensual willingness to trust. Don’t worry - we'll still have timelines, deliverables, and super-fun strategy sessions.

I’ve had the honor of working on four projects so far in the Gift. We’ve exchanged branding and websites for cash, handmade beds, carved signs, beer, and bread. I know. It feels weird. It takes guts to take a different path.
